Creates and transmits packets across the network. Deals with the physical and data link aspects of network communication. Includes protocols and hardware related to physical connection such as Ethernet, [[Wi-Fi (Wireless Fidelity)]], Hubs, Modems, Cables & wiring and manages addressing of devices on a local network. The [[ARP (address resolution protocol)]] is part of the network access layer. Security concerns in this layer involve protecting against physical access and unauthorized device connections.
